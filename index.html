A Guide to Tuition Exemption for Texas Firefighters

The Power of Texas Education Code ยง54.353

This state law authorizes the Firefighters Enrolled in Fire Science Courses program, providing a full exemption from tuition and lab fees for eligible firefighters pursuing approved fire science curricula at public institutions.

Eligibility: Who Qualifies?

The program serves both paid and volunteer firefighters who meet specific criteria.

Paid Firefighters

  • Must be employed as a paid, full-time firefighter.
  • Employer must be a political subdivision of Texas (e.g., city, county).

Volunteer Firefighters

  • Must be an active member for at least one year.
  • Department must participate in a state-approved retirement system.
  • Must hold an Accredited Advanced cert from SFFMA or a Phase V (Firefighter II) cert from TCFP.

Academic Requirements

  • Must enroll in courses within an approved Fire Science curriculum.
  • Must maintain Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP).
